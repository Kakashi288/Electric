Our Services

Expert Electric - We're Ready When You Are!

Expert Electric are dedicated to providing top-notch electrical solutions tailored to meet your needs.

We offer a range of services from electrical repairs, upgrades, and maintenance to new installations. Safety is paramount to us, we work in a way that strives to create a safe environment for every client.

Our comprehensive services include:

Domestic Installation

Our domestic installations service covers a wide range of home electrical needs to ensure your living space is safe, functional, and up-to-date.

We ensure that your electrical system meets the highest safety standards and accommodates your current and future needs.

Home Wiring and Re-wiring:

Whether you're building a new home or upgrading an older property, we provide high-quality wiring solutions that meet all current standards and regulations. Our expert electricians handle everything from planning and installation to testing and certification, guaranteeing a seamless and efficient service.

Consumer Unit Installation and Upgrades:

Keep your home’s electrical system safe and efficient with our consumer unit installation and upgrade services. We install modern consumer units (fuse boxes) equipped with the latest safety features, including RCD protection. Upgrading an outdated consumer unit can prevent electrical hazards, enhance system performance, and ensure compliance with current safety standards.

Socket-outlets and Switches:

Enhance the functionality and aesthetics of your home with our socket-outlet and switch installation services. Whether you need additional outlets, modern switch designs, or USB-integrated sockets, we provide tailored solutions to meet your needs. Our electricians ensure that all installations are carried out safely and adhere to the highest standards of quality.

Domestic Electrical Equipment and Entertainment:

We offer a comprehensive range of services for the installation and maintenance of domestic electrical equipment and entertainment systems. Our team is skilled in installing ceiling fans, light fixtures, and setting up home entertainment systems to ensure a seamless and enjoyable experience. Whether it’s a simple fixture or a complex entertainment setup, we’ve got you covered.

Smart Technology:

Transform your living space with our smart home services. We specialize in the installation and integration of smart home technologies, including automated lighting, smart thermostats, security systems, and home entertainment controls. Our experts design and implement customized smart home solutions that enhance convenience, security, and energy efficiency, making your home smarter and more connected.

Upgrades and Maintainence:

Regular upgrades and maintenance are essential to keep your electrical systems operating efficiently and safely. We offer a range of services, including updating old wiring, upgrading lighting fixtures, and enhancing your electrical infrastructure. Our preventive maintenance services help identify and address potential issues before they become major problems, ensuring the longevity and safety of your electrical systems.

Troubleshooting and Repairs:

When electrical issues arise, our troubleshooting and repair services are here to help. Our experienced electricians quickly diagnose and resolve a wide range of problems, from flickering lights and faulty outlets to circuit breaker issues and complete power outages. We use advanced diagnostic tools to identify the root cause and provide effective solutions, restoring your electrical systems to optimal performance.

Commercial Installation

Our commercial electrical installations are designed to meet the demanding needs of businesses and commercial properties.

We provide comprehensive solutions that ensure your electrical systems are reliable, efficient, and compliant with industry standards.

Wiring systems:

Wiring systems and dedicated circuits for heavy-duty commercial equipment, structured cabling systems for data networks, including Ethernet cables, fiber optics, and data jacks ensuring they receive sufficient power and are safely connected.

Lighting Systems:

Installing and maintaining general lighting systems for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial environments. This includes overhead lighting, recessed lighting, and track lighting.

Distribution Board Installation and Upgrades:

Installing and maintaining the main distribution board that manages the electrical supply throughout the building to installing sub panels to distribute power to different areas or floors within the commercial property, ensuring an efficient and balanced power supply.

Routine Maintenance:

Routine maintenance for commercial properties involves regular inspections, testing, and cleaning of electrical systems to ensure safety, efficiency, and compliance with regulations. This includes visual inspections, testing, and tightening connections.

Troubleshooting and Repairs:

Identifying and resolving electrical issues to ensure safety and efficiency. This includes diagnosing faults in wiring, outlets, switches, and equipment, and addressing problems such as power outages, tripped circuits, and faulty connections. Repairs may involve replacing damaged components, rewiring, and updating electrical panels to meet current standards. Regular troubleshooting helps prevent future issues, ensuring a reliable and safe electrical system for your commercial property.

Smoke and Heat Alarms

Safety is paramount in any home or business, and having reliable smoke and heat alarms is essential for early detection of fire hazards.

Our comprehensive smoke and heat alarm installation services ensure that your property is protected with the latest technology and optimal placement for maximum effectiveness.

Smoke Alarm Installation:

Proper installation of smoke alarms in key areas of your home or business, ensuring early detection of smoke and fire. We install both ionization and photoelectric smoke alarms to provide comprehensive coverage.

Heat Alarm Installation:

Ideal for areas where smoke alarms may not be suitable, such as kitchens and garages. Heat alarms detect rapid increases in temperature, providing an additional layer of safety.

Interconnected Systems:

Installation of interconnected alarm systems, where all alarms sound simultaneously when one is triggered, ensuring that everyone in the building is alerted to potential danger.

Battery and Hardwired Options:

We offer both battery-operated and hardwired alarms with battery backup, ensuring continuous operation even during power outages.

Regular Maintenance and Testing:

Ongoing maintenance services, including testing and battery replacement, to ensure your alarms are always in working order.

Electrical Waste

Proper disposal and management of electrical waste is crucial for environmental sustainability and public health.

We offer comprehensive electrical waste management services that ensure your old and unused electrical equipment is donated or disposed of responsibly and recycled efficiently.

By choosing our electrical waste management services, you contribute to a cleaner environment and a more sustainable future.

Donate Electrical Items:

Donating electrical items to charity is a great way to help those in need while promoting environmental sustainability. Ensure the items are in good working condition, clean, and include necessary accessories. Some charities offer pickup services for larger items, while others require you to drop off the donations at their locations, our team can arrange these collections.

Collection and Disposal:

Our team provides hassle-free collection and disposal services for all types of electrical waste, including old appliances, broken devices, and outdated equipment. We ensure safe and compliant disposal.

Recycling and Reclamation:

We prioritize recycling to minimize the impact on landfills and the environment. Our process involves dismantling devices, sorting components, and extracting valuable materials like metals, plastics, and rare earth elements. These materials are then processed and reused, contributing to the circular economy.

Sustainable Practises:

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond recycling. We also provide advice on how to reduce electrical waste through proper maintenance, repair, and upgrades of your existing equipment. By prolonging the life of your devices, you can contribute to reducing waste and conserving resources.

Electrical Certification

Electrical certification is crucial to ensure that your property's electrical systems are safe, compliant, and functioning correctly.

Our comprehensive electrical certification services provide peace of mind by verifying that all electrical installations meet the required standards and regulations. Whether you are a homeowner, landlord, or business owner, our team of qualified electricians is here to help.

Safety Inspections:

Thorough safety inspections to identify any potential electrical hazards. We check all wiring, outlets, switches, and appliances to ensure they are safe and functioning correctly.

Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICR):

Detailed reports that assess the condition of your electrical installations. An EICR is essential for identifying any deficiencies and ensuring compliance with the latest electrical standards.

Landlord Certifications:

Compliance checks for rental properties to ensure they meet legal requirements. We provide Electrical Safety Certificates for landlords, helping them to meet their obligations and keep tenants safe.

PAT Testing:

Portable Appliance Testing (PAT) to ensure that all electrical appliances are safe to use. Regular PAT testing is essential for businesses to comply with health and safety regulations.

New Installation Certification:

Certification for new electrical installations, including residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

Expert Advice and Consultation:

Professional advice and consultation to help you understand the certification requirements and ensure your property is compliant.

Renewable Energy

Renewable energy sources are crucial in addressing the challenges of climate change and reducing our dependence on fossil fuels.

They are derived from natural processes that are replenished constantly, such as sunlight, wind, and geothermal heat. One of the most popular and effective forms of renewable energy is solar power, which converts sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) cells.

The Solar Installation process:

1. Initial Consultation and Site Assessment
2. Installation
3. Inspection and Testing
4. Activation

Solar Installation:

Solar installation is a smart investment in both the environment and your future. By harnessing the power of the sun, you can enjoy significant cost savings, contribute to a cleaner planet, and increase your property’s value. Whether for residential or commercial use, solar energy offers a sustainable solution that benefits both you and the environment.
